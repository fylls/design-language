The main compositional element, the tangled lettering, follows a strong diagonal axis.

There is also a lot of movement in this lettering:
the words are intertwining with each other, and the whole mass is moving upwards at a diagonal.
This tangly headline text contrasts to the rigid verticals and horizontals of the gridded information section above it.
These are two very different approaches to alignment and movement in the same poster.

Contrast
We can see multiple elements of strong visual contrast, most prominently:
scale (the large, tangled lettering versus the very small information at the top)
and line or outline (the tangly, weaving shapes of the headline lettering contrasted to the geometric,
architectural grid-like shapes in the information section at the top)


Stress
The tangled lettering in this poster seem to be pushing up to the right,
in a strong diagonal that is putting greater weight in the upper right part of the poster.
The elements are not evenly balanced in the space of the poster.
