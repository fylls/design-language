This is poster is challenging (you could also argue that it is illegible). 

It is largely illegible, but there are key areas that are legible enough to be read. 

For example, the top of the poster is highly legible,
which provides enough information to indicate what this poster was designed for and what it is advertising.
The rest of the poster is mainly layered type and graphic elements that are illegible and don’t convey clear information pertinent to the event. 

It is difficult to tell, but the designers deliberately designed this poster by layering several other posters they had designed for these events.
The designers designed a kind of system of posters, that were carefully calculated to produce a kind of master poster that made some areas very
illegible and visually evocative, yet left the basic information clear and easy to read.
